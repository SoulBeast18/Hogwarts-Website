The Four Houses of Hogwarts

At Hogwarts, students are sorted into one of four houses, each with its own noble history, outstanding characteristics, and notable alumni. Your house will be like your family - you'll share classes, dormitories, and the common room with your housemates.

Gryffindor

"Where dwell the brave at heart"

House Characteristics

  • Colors: Scarlet and Gold
  • Element: Fire
  • Animal: Lion
  • Ghost: Nearly Headless Nick
  • Founder: Godric Gryffindor
  • Head of House: Minerva McGonagall

Values

  • Courage
  • Bravery
  • Determination
  • Chivalry
  • Daring

Gryffindor values bravery, daring, nerve, and chivalry. Its emblematic animal is the lion, and its colors are scarlet and gold. Gryffindor corresponds roughly to the element of fire. The common room is located in one of the highest towers at Hogwarts.

Notable Alumni

  • Harry Potter
  • Hermione Granger
  • Ron Weasley
  • Albus Dumbledore

Hufflepuff

"Where they are just and loyal"

House Characteristics

  • Colors: Yellow and Black
  • Element: Earth
  • Animal: Badger
  • Ghost: Fat Friar
  • Founder: Helga Hufflepuff
  • Head of House: Pomona Sprout

Values

  • Hard work
  • Dedication
  • Patience
  • Loyalty
  • Fair play

Hufflepuff is the most inclusive among the four houses; valuing hard work, dedication, patience, loyalty, and fair play. The house has produced its share of great wizards â€“ including Newt Scamander and Nymphadora Tonks.

Notable Alumni

  • Newt Scamander
  • Cedric Diggory
  • Nymphadora Tonks
  • Pomona Sprout

Ravenclaw

"Wit beyond measure is man's greatest treasure"

House Characteristics

  • Colors: Blue and Bronze
  • Element: Air
  • Animal: Eagle
  • Ghost: Grey Lady
  • Founder: Rowena Ravenclaw
  • Head of House: Filius Flitwick

Values

  • Intelligence
  • Wisdom
  • Creativity
  • Learning
  • Wit

Ravenclaw values intelligence, creativity, learning, and wit. Many well-known wizards have come from this house, including Filius Flitwick and Luna Lovegood. Ravenclaws are known for their unique perspective on the world.

Notable Alumni

  • Luna Lovegood
  • Cho Chang
  • Filius Flitwick
  • Garrick Ollivander

Slytherin

"Those cunning folk use any means to achieve their ends"

House Characteristics

  • Colors: Green and Silver
  • Element: Water
  • Animal: Serpent
  • Ghost: Bloody Baron
  • Founder: Salazar Slytherin
  • Head of House: Severus Snape

Values

  • Ambition
  • Cunning
  • Leadership
  • Resourcefulness
  • Determination

Slytherin house values ambition, cunning, leadership, and resourcefulness. The house has produced many great witches and wizards, including Merlin himself. Slytherins are known for their strong bonds of loyalty to their fellow house members.

Notable Alumni

  • Merlin
  • Severus Snape
  • Horace Slughorn
  • Andromeda Tonks
