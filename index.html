The Four Houses

Gryffindor

"Where dwell the brave at heart." Gryffindor values courage, bravery, nerve, and chivalry.

Founded by Godric Gryffindor

Slytherin

"Those cunning folk use any means to achieve their ends." Slytherin values ambition, cunning, leadership, and resourcefulness.

Founded by Salazar Slytherin

Ravenclaw

"Wit beyond measure is man's greatest treasure." Ravenclaw values intelligence, creativity, learning, and wit.

Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw

Hufflepuff

"Those patient Hufflepuffs are true and unafraid of toil." Hufflepuff values hard work, dedication, patience, and loyalty.

Founded by Helga Hufflepuff
